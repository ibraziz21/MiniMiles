AkibaMiles Office Hours. Today's question is: what are AkibaMiles?

Short answer: AkibaMiles are reward points inside the AkibaMiles app.

You earn them by completing challenges, playing skill games, and joining supported activities in the app.

Then you can use Miles for things like games, eligible raffles, and reward opportunities when they are live.

So the loop is simple: earn Miles, use Miles, and track your progress in one place.
