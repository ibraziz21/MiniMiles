# DESIGN.md - AkibaMiles Farkle Coming Soon

## Style Prompt

Dark premium motion graphics for an AkibaMiles game teaser. The opening is a quiet navy-to-teal brand reveal with no clutter, slow confidence, and a centered logo build. The ending is pure black with heavy white kinetic type for "FARKLE" and a softer "coming soon" line.

## Colors

- `#04111F` - deep navy base background.
- `#062236` - cool midnight transition shade.
- `#063A41` - deep teal shadow.
- `#238D9D` - AkibaMiles primary teal for the circular logo mark.
- `#F8FFFF` - tinted white for high-contrast typography.

## Typography

- Logo wordmark: Avenir Next or Helvetica Neue, 800 weight, clean sans-serif.
- Farkle title: Arial Black or Avenir Next Heavy fallback, 900 weight, ultra-heavy sans-serif.
- Secondary line: Avenir Next or Helvetica Neue, 500 weight.

## What NOT to Do

- No avatar, no narration, no sound bed.
- No stock imagery, UI mockups, confetti, or extra game explanation.
- No busy particle fields or decorative clutter.
- No gradient text; all text stays solid white.
- No rounded cards or framed containers.
